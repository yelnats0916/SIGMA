FAQ

What is my ID and Email?

Your official ID and Email are provided upon enrollment. If you need to retrieve these credentials, please visit the Registrar's Office with a valid ID.

What is the default password?

Default passwords are typically your Surname followed by your registration id given in elms by the admin. If you are still unsure of your password details, please contact the Registrar's Office or your designated ELMS administrator.

How do I reset a forgotten password?

For security reasons, password resets are handled in person. Please visit the Admin Office to request a manual reset of your account credentials.

Why is my account inactive?

Accounts may be set to inactive if there are pending enrollment issues or if the account hasn't been used for two consecutive semesters.

Contact Support

When should I contact support?

Reach out if you still cannot sign in, your account details do not match, your password reset does not work, or you need help confirming enrollment-related access.

Where can I ask for help?

You may visit the ICT Department during school hours, or open the official contact page for registrar and school support concerns.
